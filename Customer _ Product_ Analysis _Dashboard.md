Customer & Product Analysis Dashboard

Dashboard Overview:
The Customer & Product Analysis Dashboard provides detailed insights into customer purchasing behavior and product category performance. 
It enables business stakeholders to identify high-value customers, evaluate customer rankings, analyze product category contributions, and compare historical sales performance. 
The dashboard supports customer-focused strategies and product portfolio optimization through interactive visualizations.

Dashboard Purpose:
The purpose of this dashboard is to analyze customer and product performance, identify key revenue contributors, evaluate historical sales trends, and support strategic decision-making
related to customer engagement and product management.

Business Objectives:
Identify top revenue-generating customers.
Evaluate customer rankings based on sales performance.
Analyze product category contribution to total sales.
Compare previous year sales performance.
Support customer segmentation and product portfolio optimization.
Enable data-driven marketing and sales strategies.

Data Source:
Dataset: Brazlian E-Commerece public Dataset by Olist
Reporting Period: 2016 – 2018

Dashboard Features:
Top Customers by Sales
Customer Ranking Analysis
Previous Year Sales Waterfall Analysis
Product Category Treemap
Interactive Year Filter
Customer City Slicer

Business Analysis:

1. Top Customer Sales Analysis
Key Insight
The dashboard identifies the highest revenue-generating customers based on total sales, highlighting those who contribute significantly to overall business revenue.

Business Interpretation
A relatively small group of customers contributes a substantial portion of sales. Recognizing these high-value customers enables the business to strengthen customer relationships and improve retention strategies.

Strategic Recommendation
Develop loyalty programs for high-value customers.
Implement personalized marketing campaigns.

2. Customer Ranking Analysis
Key Insight
Customers are ranked according to their sales performance, providing a clear comparison of individual customer contributions.

Business Interpretation
Customer ranking helps identify the most valuable customers while also revealing opportunities to engage mid-tier and lower-performing customer segments.

Strategic Recommendation
Segment customers based on purchasing behavior.
Design targeted retention strategies for top-ranked customers.
Launch promotional campaigns to increase engagement among lower-ranked customers.
Regularly review customer rankings to monitor changes over time.

3. Previous Year Sales Analysis
Key Insight
The waterfall chart illustrates how monthly sales accumulated throughout the previous year, ultimately contributing to the annual total.

Business Interpretation
The visualization highlights the cumulative impact of monthly sales performance, enabling stakeholders to evaluate historical growth patterns and identify periods of
stronger or weaker business activity.

Strategic Recommendation
Compare historical performance with current-year sales.
Investigate months with significant changes in revenue.
Use historical trends to improve future sales forecasting.
Support strategic planning through year-over-year comparisons.

4. Product Category Performance
Key Insight
The treemap visualization indicates that product categories such as Beleza & Saúde, Relógios Presentes, Cama Mesa Banho, Esporte Lazer, and Informática Acessórios contribute 
significantly to total sales.

Business Interpretation
Sales performance is concentrated within a limited number of product categories, highlighting their importance to overall business success. Lower-performing categories may require
additional attention to improve their contribution.

Strategic Recommendation
Prioritize inventory and promotional activities for top-performing categories.
Evaluate pricing strategies for underperforming categories.
Expand product offerings in high-demand categories.
Monitor category performance regularly to support inventory optimization.

5. Customer and Product Performance Overview
Key Insight
Combining customer and product analysis provides a comprehensive understanding of revenue generation by identifying both high-value customers and high-performing product categories.

Business Interpretation
Integrating customer behavior with product performance enables better business decisions related to customer engagement, product assortment, and sales strategy.

Strategic Recommendation
Combine customer segmentation with product recommendations.
Develop personalized marketing campaigns based on purchasing patterns.
Identify opportunities to increase revenue through targeted promotions.
Use customer insights to optimize product placement and merchandising strategies.

Summary of Findings:
A limited number of customers generate a significant share of total revenue.
Customer ranking enables the identification of high-value and growth-potential customer segments.
Historical sales analysis supports long-term business performance evaluation.
Several product categories contribute disproportionately to overall sales.
Combining customer and product analysis provides valuable insights for strategic decision-making.
Interactive filtering enhances business analysis across different years and customer locations.

Business Value:
The Customer & Product Analysis Dashboard provides valuable insights into customer behavior and product performance, enabling organizations to identify key revenue contributors,
improve customer retention, optimize product portfolios, and support data-driven business strategies. By combining customer analytics with product performance metrics, the dashboard 
helps management make informed decisions that enhance profitability and customer satisfaction.
