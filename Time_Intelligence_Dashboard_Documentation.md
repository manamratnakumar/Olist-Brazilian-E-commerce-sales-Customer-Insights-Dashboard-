Dashboard Overview:
The Time Intelligence Dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance over time by leveraging advanced DAX time intelligence calculations. 
It enables business stakeholders to evaluate Year-to-Date (YTD), Month-to-Date (MTD), Quarter-to-Date (QTD), Running Total, Rolling 12-Month Sales, and Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth.
This dashboard supports trend analysis, performance monitoring, and strategic planning using historical sales data.

Dashboard Purpose:
The purpose of this dashboard is to monitor sales performance across different time periods, identify long-term business trends, evaluate historical growth, and support data-driven
forecasting through advanced time intelligence metrics.

Business Objectives:
Monitor sales performance using time-based KPIs.
Compare current sales with historical performance.
Evaluate Year-to-Date, Month-to-Date, and Quarter-to-Date sales.
Analyze cumulative sales trends using Running Total.
Measure long-term sales performance using Rolling 12-Month analysis.
Track Year-over-Year growth to evaluate business progress.

Data Source:
Dataset: Brazilian E-Commerce public Dataset by olist 
Reporting Period: 2016 – 2018

 Business Analysis:
1. Year-to-Date (YTD) Sales Performance
Key Insight
The business generated 7.57M in Year-to-Date sales during the selected reporting period.

Business Interpretation
The YTD metric provides a cumulative view of annual sales performance, enabling stakeholders to monitor progress toward yearly business targets and evaluate overall revenue generation.

Strategic Recommendation
Monitor YTD performance against annual sales goals.
Compare YTD results across multiple years to identify long-term trends.
Use YTD analysis to support strategic planning and budgeting.

2. Running Total Sales Analysis
Key Insight
The Running Total chart illustrates cumulative sales over time, showing a gradual decline throughout the reporting period.

Business Interpretation
The downward trend indicates that cumulative sales growth is slowing. Monitoring running totals helps identify shifts in business performance and supports long-term operational planning.

Strategic Recommendation
Continuously monitor cumulative sales performance.
Investigate periods of slower growth.
Align inventory and marketing strategies with long-term sales trends.

3. Rolling 12-Month Sales Trend
Key Insight
Rolling 12-Month sales demonstrate a consistent downward trend over the reporting period.

Business Interpretation
Rolling 12-Month analysis smooths short-term fluctuations, providing a clearer view of long-term business performance. The observed decline suggests sustained changes in sales 
patterns that require further analysis.

Strategic Recommendation
Use Rolling 12-Month metrics for forecasting and strategic planning.
Identify seasonal patterns and recurring sales cycles.
Evaluate long-term customer purchasing behavior.

4. Year-over-Year Growth Analysis:
Key Insight
The dashboard reports an overall Year-over-Year Growth of 2.35%, while the monthly YoY growth trend shows a sharp increase at the beginning of the period followed by relatively
stable growth levels.

Business Interpretation
Although overall YoY growth remains positive, the monthly trend suggests that growth is not evenly distributed across the reporting period. Additional analysis is required to 
understand the factors influencing these fluctuations.

Strategic Recommendation
Monitor YoY performance on a monthly basis.
Compare growth across regions, products, and customer segments.
Investigate significant increases or declines to identify improvement opportunities.

5. Month-to-Date (MTD) and Quarter-to-Date (QTD) Performance
Key Insight
Both MTD and QTD metrics report 275.40, providing a snapshot of current sales performance within the selected month and quarter.

Business Interpretation
These metrics allow business users to track short-term performance and quickly determine whether current sales are aligned with operational targets.

Strategic Recommendation
Review MTD and QTD metrics regularly to monitor short-term business performance.
Compare current values with historical periods to identify emerging trends.
Use these metrics to support tactical business decisions.
