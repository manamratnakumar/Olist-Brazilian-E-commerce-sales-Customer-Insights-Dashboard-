Executive Dashboard

Dashboard Overview: The Executive Summary Dashboard provides a high-level overview of the company's sales performance, customer activity, and operational KPIs. 
It enables executives to monitor business health, identify trends, and make strategic decisions through interactive visualizations.

Business Problem: Senior management lacked a centralized reporting system to monitor overall business performance. Decision-makers needed a dashboard that provides instant 
visibility into sales, customers, orders, and product performance to support strategic planning.

Business Objectives: Monitor overall sales performance. Track key business KPIs. Analyze monthly sales trends. Compare current sales with previous year.
Identify top-performing product categories. Monitor sales across different cities.

Business Questions & Insights

Q1. What is the overall business performance?

Insight:

Total Sales: 13.59M Total Orders: 99K Total Customers: 99K Average Sales per Order: 137.75

Business Action:

Continue monitoring executive KPIs to ensure business growth and identify performance gaps early.

Q2. Is sales increasing or decreasing over time?

Insight:

Monthly sales indicate seasonal fluctuations with noticeable declines during certain months.

Business Action:

Plan promotional campaigns during low-performing periods to stabilize sales.

Q3. Which product categories contribute the most revenue?

Insight:

Beauty & Health, Sports & Leisure, Watches & Gifts, and Home & Living generate the highest revenue.

Business Action:

Prioritize inventory planning and marketing investments for high-performing categories.

Q4. Which cities contribute the highest sales?

Insight:

Sales vary significantly across customer locations, highlighting high-performing markets.

Business Action:

Expand marketing efforts in top-performing cities while improving performance in low-sales regions.

Q5. How does current performance compare with previous year?

Insight:

Sales trends closely follow previous-year patterns, indicating recurring seasonality.

Business Action:

Use historical trends for sales forecasting and budgeting.

Key KPIs:

Total Sales Total Orders Total Customers Average Sales per Order Monthly Sales Previous Year Sales

Key Business Insights:

Generated 13.59M revenue. Processed 99K customer orders. Average order value reached 137.75. Seasonal trends influence monthly sales performance. A few product categories drive 
the majority of revenue.

Business Recommendations:

Focus on top-selling product categories. Launch seasonal marketing campaigns. Improve sales performance in underperforming cities. Monitor KPIs regularly using executive dashboards.

Business Value:

Provided executives with a centralized dashboard for monitoring business performance, enabling faster and more informed strategic decisions.

Dashboard Overview:

Time Intelligence Dashboard

The Time Intelligence Dashboard provides advanced time-based analysis, enabling stakeholders to monitor sales performance using Year-to-Date (YTD), Month-to-Date (MTD), 
Quarter-to-Date (QTD), Running Total, Rolling 12-Month trends, and Year-over-Year (YoY) comparisons.

Business Problem:

Business leaders needed a dynamic reporting solution to analyze historical sales performance, monitor business growth, identify seasonal trends, and support accurate forecasting
using time-based metrics.

Business Objectives:

Monitor YTD, MTD, and QTD performance. Analyze Year-over-Year growth. Track cumulative sales trends. Identify seasonal sales patterns. Improve forecasting accuracy.

Business Questions & Insights:

Q1. What is the current Year-to-Date sales?

Insight:

Current Year-to-Date sales reached 7.57M.

Business Action:

Compare YTD performance against annual targets and monitor progress regularly.

Q2. How is business performing over time?

Insight:

Running Total analysis demonstrates cumulative sales growth throughout the reporting period.

Business Action:

Monitor cumulative sales to ensure business objectives remain on track.

Q3. Is the business growing compared with last year?

Insight:

Year-over-Year analysis identifies periods of positive and negative sales growth.

Business Action:

Investigate months with declining performance and adjust business strategies accordingly.

Q4. What does the Rolling 12-Month trend indicate?

Insight:

Rolling averages smooth seasonal fluctuations and reveal long-term sales trends.

Business Action:

Use rolling trends to improve forecasting, budgeting, and inventory planning.

Q5. Which states contribute the most sales?

Insight:

State-level analysis highlights regional performance differences across the business.

Business Action:

Increase investment in high-performing regions while developing growth strategies for underperforming states.

Key KPIs:

Sales YTD Sales MTD Sales QTD Previous Year Sales Running Total Rolling 12-Month Sales Year-over-Year Growth

Key Business Insights:

Achieved 7.57M in Year-to-Date sales. Running Total provides visibility into cumulative growth. Rolling 12-Month trends support long-term planning. 
YoY analysis identifies seasonal growth patterns. Regional analysis supports location-based business strategies.

Business Recommendations:

Monitor YTD and YoY performance regularly. Use rolling trends for demand forecasting. Optimize inventory based on seasonal demand. Improve sales strategies in underperforming regions. 
Review monthly performance to achieve annual targets.

Business Value:

Delivered advanced Time Intelligence reporting that enables proactive monitoring of business performance, improves forecasting accuracy, and supports strategic planning through 
historical and comparative sales analysis.
