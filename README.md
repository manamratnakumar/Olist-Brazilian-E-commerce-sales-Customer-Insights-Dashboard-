Strong Overall Revenue Generation

Business Impact: The company demonstrates healthy revenue generation and operational efficiency.

Average Order Value Indicates Stable Customer Spending
The Average Sales per Order is 137.75, suggesting customers consistently purchase products with a reasonable transaction value.

Business Impact: Opportunities exist to further increase revenue through cross-selling, upselling, and bundled product offerings.

Sales Performance Shows a Declining Trend
Monthly sales trends reveal a gradual decline over the reporting period, indicating reduced sales momentum.

Business Impact: Continuous monitoring is required to identify whether the decline is driven by seasonality, changing customer demand, or competitive market conditions.

Product categories such as Beleza & Saúde, Relógios Presentes, Cama Mesa Banho, Esporte Lazer, and Informática Acessórios contribute a significant portion of overall revenue.

Business Impact: Focusing inventory, marketing, and promotional strategies on these categories can maximize profitability while improving the performance of lower-contributing categories.

Positive Year-over-Year Business Growth
The dashboard reports an overall Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth of 2.35%, indicating that business performance has improved compared to the previous year.

Business Impact: Despite declining monthly trends, the business continues to maintain positive annual growth.

Time Intelligence Supports Better Business Planning
Advanced DAX calculations such as YTD, MTD, QTD, Running Total, and Rolling 12-Month Sales provide multiple perspectives for evaluating business performance.

Business Impact: These metrics enable management to monitor short-term progress while supporting long-term forecasting and strategic planning.

High-Value Customers Drive Business Performance
Customer analysis highlights that a relatively small group of customers contributes a substantial share of total revenue.

Business Impact: Retaining these customers through loyalty programs and personalized engagement can significantly improve long-term business growth.

Customer Ranking Enables Better Segmentation
Ranking customers based on sales performance helps identify premium customers as well as those with growth potential.

Business Impact: Customer segmentation allows the business to implement targeted marketing campaigns and improve customer lifetime value.

Historical Sales Analysis Supports Forecasting
Historical sales comparisons and Running Total analysis provide valuable insights into long-term business performance.

Business Impact: Understanding historical trends improves forecasting accuracy, inventory planning, and resource allocation.

Interactive Dashboards Improve Decision-Making
Interactive filters such as Year and Customer Location allow stakeholders to explore sales performance across different business dimensions.

Business Impact: Decision-makers can quickly identify trends, compare performance, and make data-driven business decisions without manual reporting.

Overall Business Conclusion:

The Power BI solution provides a comprehensive view of business performance by integrating executive KPIs, time intelligence analysis, customer behavior, and product performance into a single interactive reporting platform. The dashboards reveal that while the business has achieved 13.59M in sales and maintained 2.35% YoY growth, declining monthly sales trends highlight the need for proactive strategies focused on customer retention, category optimization, and demand forecasting.
